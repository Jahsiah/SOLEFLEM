Main Features

A modern smart handle with a touch keypad and a fingerprint reader is suitable for use in both right and left doors, in the place of installation of a traditional handle.
It can be opened by entering the user's PIN code, scanning the previously entered fingerprint or using the bluetooth connection and the application on the phone.
The included fixing screws allow for installation in doors with a thickness of 38mm to 58mm, and the elegant shape of the handle fits many types of doors.
The handle is available in two versions: black or nickel-plated.
